How To BID

Here at BIDSART our aim has always been to provide a platform that Encourages Artist, sculptors,craftworkers of our Country and get them the recognition they deserve. In India the audience base of various Art Form's is immensily huge but only few appreciate the artists in a way which motivate's the artist to follow their Passion and make living out of it. Our goal is to reach these artists and support them in a way which motivates them to carry forward their Art Work.

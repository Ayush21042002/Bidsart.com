About Us

Here at BIDSART our aim has always been to provide a platform that Encourages Artist, sculptors,craftworkers of our Country and get them the recognition they deserve. In India the audience base of various Art Form's is immensily huge but only few appreciate the artists in a way which motivate's the artist to follow their Passion and make living out of it. Our goal is to reach these artists and support them in a way which motivates them to carry forward their Art Work.

We were mostly inspired by eBay and the website called artsy. Now there are many e-commerce sites(In case of India, majorly amazon and flipkart ) out there, but few focuses exclusively on the artist and sculptors and due to the variety of products on these sites, the artists generally gets overshadowed. But we are different from others!!!!

We offer exclusivity to the user. And we will focus more on the artists and craftworkers. And rather than only specifying the selling price of product, we will also give the users a chance to bid in an auction for it. So our main focus will be on auctioning these products. WHAT will you do to make e-auction more thrilling??? We will have LIVE AUCTIONS on the weekends. So that a person can show off their bidding skills and experience fun and thrill at the same time.
